Our Story

Luxury Defined by the Sea

MonteYacht has been crafting unforgettable maritime experiences along the Adriatic coast since 2010. Based in Porto Montenegro — one of the Mediterranean's most prestigious superyacht marinas — we offer curated day charters and week-long voyages along the Montenegrin coastline.

With an 18-metre yacht, an experienced captain, and a commitment to bespoke service, every charter is designed around you. Whether it's a sun-filled afternoon in Kotor Bay or a week exploring hidden coves and coastal villages, we handle every detail.
