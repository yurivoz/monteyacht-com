Six hours at sea, between Tivat and the open Adriatic. We leave at 17:00, when the light starts to soften. By 19:30 we're anchored in a calm bay south of Verige — champagne on deck, music low enough that you still hear the water.

A white dress code, twelve guests, one yacht. The whole evening built around the hour the sun goes down.

"This is the night we wait for all winter — the first time the season feels real." — Captain Alexei

Limited to twelve guests. We confirm reservations within two hours.
